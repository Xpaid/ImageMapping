Quezon Province

Quezon, a sprawling province located southeast of Luzon Island in the Philippines, is a land of breathtaking natural beauty and rich cultural heritage. From the majestic Mount Banahaw, a sacred pilgrimage site, to the pristine beaches of Polillo Island, Quezon offers diverse landscapes for every traveler. This historically significant region, known as the birthplace of numerous Filipino heroes and revolutionaries, also boasts vibrant festivals, delectable cuisine, and welcoming locals, making it a truly unforgettable destination.
